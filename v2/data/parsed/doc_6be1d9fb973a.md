The subsidiaries of the Bank continue to follow the framework as applicable to them.

Further, in compliance with the said RBI Investment Direction 2023, the valuation gains and losses at the period ended 31st December, 2024, across all performing investments held under AFS are aggregated and the net appreciation amounting to `34.00 crores (net of tax) has been directly recognised in AFS Reserve.
